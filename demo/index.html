How PhishShield Works

Our AI analyzes multiple "signals" within an email to determine if it's a phishing attempt:

  • Urgency & Fear: Scammers use pressure to make you act without thinking.
  • Greed & Rewards: Promises of free gifts or prizes are common traps.
  • Technical Spells: We look for domain mismatches and suspicious URL structures.
  • Link Analysis: Phishing often hides behind deceptive links.
